INTRODUCTION

Over the past four years at Appwrite, I've been involved in designing experiences across multiple platforms. From the console to website pages, and even a custom physical keyboard.

My work spans user research, interface design, and usability testing, always aiming to make complex systems simple, intuitive, and efficient for developers.

THE CONSOLE

The Appwrite console is where developers manage everything from authentication to hosting. Over the years, I've worked across multiple areas of the console, including Auth, Databases, Messaging, and Sites.

Each project began with user interviews and usability testing to uncover pain points. Designs were iterated based on developer feedback and evolving product goals, in close collaboration with backend and frontend engineers.

THE WEBSITE

On the website side, I focused on creating clean, flexible, and responsive layouts that clearly communicate the product's message.

Whether designing the blog, documentation, or product pages, I ensured a consistent brand experience, collaborating closely with design and engineering teams.
