INTRODUCTION

Chameleon is an innovative online sneaker marketplace that empowers users to design custom sneakers and express their unique style.

With a wide selection of shoe models as a creative canvas, users can personalize every detail to craft one-of-a-kind designs. It also offers a collection of pre-designed sneakers, available for purchase, inspiration, or as a starting point for customization.

THE PROBLEM

Personalization is powerful. When you receive a gift that's thoughtfully tailored to you, it feels more meaningful because it's uniquely yours.

Through user interviews, I discovered that consumers are increasingly aware of their desires and preferences, yet often feel that mainstream products fall short of meeting their needs.

ChameleonUSER'S PROFILE

Users can easily access their saved designs, uploads, and liked items.

Additionally, Chameleon offers a personalized dashboard with key information, including earned points from purchases and design views (which can be redeemed for discounts on future purchases), as well as popular designs available for purchase or as inspiration.
