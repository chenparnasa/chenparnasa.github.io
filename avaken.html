INTRODUCTION

Avaken is a company that combines innovative solar energy technologies and smart home technology.


Avaken's smarthome app is for private consumers who have installed solar panels on their roof and use some of the collected energy for home use.

THE PROBLEM

The system enables operation and control of the smart home infrastructure and electrical products connected to it. Using the system, the user can monitor the balance of energy accumulated and utilized from the solar system.


While designing the app's interface, it was important to note that its use was possible from anywhere at any time.

AvakenFOR MOBILE

It was necessary to adapt the app to a smartphone environment as well, so I made adjustments while maintaining that the usage is the same and the app can be used for the same purposes.

AvakenFOR DESKTOP

Avaken also offers a dashboard that provides information on power consumption by category. This software is accessed only through a desktop site and is designed according to the smart home app.
