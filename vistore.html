THE IDEA

As part of this project, I analyzed and enhanced an existing design language, focusing on key elements such as color, typography, forms, iconography, and effects.

The next phase involved applying the expanded design language for a cross-platform e-commerce application, ensuring a cohesive and visually engaging user experience across devices.

ORIGINAL SCREENS

The original screens featured a dark mode design with stroke icons, rounded corners, and bold typography. The color palette includes shades of orange, blue, and purple, with orange highlighting call-to-action elements.
